    PLASMA SHOCK PEENING (PSP)


    We develop the shockwaves generator device. The shockwaves cause plastic deformations in metal material surface layer leading to impartment of beneficial compressive residual stresses.â€‹

    The application of PSP is envisaged in the engineering production, wherever compressive residual stresses increase resistance of metals to surface-related failures such as fatigue, stress corrosion cracking, fretting fatigue, wear, cavitation and has other benefits and applications.

    The PSP technology can thus extend the life of metal parts and increase their operational reliability and safety, which, in turn, can prevent costly repairs following catastrophic failures.

    We bring fully fledged results to laser shock peening (LSP).
