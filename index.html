    ABOUT US

    Driven by Technology

    PSP Technologies is a technological spin-off company from Institute of Termomechanics, Academy of Science Czech Republic, with ambition to change the shot peening market. Our technology has worldwide protection and wide range of applications in material engineering. By enhancing surface residual stress we
    increase the lifetime of metal parts. The treated surface is made compacter and denser.

    PSP Technologies has a team of experts in new technology development, design and business with international ambitions.


    PLASMA SHOCK PEENING (PSP)


    We develop the shockwaves generator device. The shockwaves cause plastic deformations in metal material surface layer leading to impartment of beneficial compressive residual stresses.​

    The application of PSP is envisaged in the engineering production, wherever compressive residual stresses increase resistance of metals to surface-related failures such as fatigue, stress corrosion cracking, fretting fatigue, wear, cavitation and has other benefits and applications.

    The PSP technology can thus extend the life of metal parts and increase their operational reliability and safety, which, in turn, can prevent costly repairs following catastrophic failures.

    We bring fully fledged results to laser shock peening (LSP).

    PSP ADDED VALUE

    flexibility

    Wide range of application

    Plasma shock peening don't need direct optical access to treated surface. More over, technology can be operated in dry and real environment without any limitation.

    Therefore we can harden internal parts of treated components, corners of complex forms and other hardly accessible parts.

    efficiency

    Extending life time of you products

    We can significantly improve the life time of clients products by adjustment of surface tension.

    We reach similar values of treated surface as Laser shock peening.

    The life time can be extended by hundreds of percent.

    low operational costs

    Increasing the productivity

    Plasma shock peening can be implemented in several manufacturing to increase the quality of production with relatively low extra cost.

    Operational costs are significantly lower than in laser shock peening.
